## Source Finding

**Article:** "Are the Mysteries of Quantum Mechanics Beginning to Dissolve?" by Philip Ball (Feb 13, 2026)

**Key Concept:** Wojciech Zurek's Quantum Darwinism theory explains how classical reality emerges from quantum mechanics:

- **Decoherence**: Quantum objects entangle with environment, "diluting" quantumness
- **Pointer States**: Quantum states that can robustly generate multiple identical imprints
- **Quantum Darwinism**: "Fit" states survive because they replicate efficiently across environment
- **Unique Classical Reality**: All imprints must be identical ‚Äî consensus reality emerges
- **Epiontic**: Wave function is both epistemic (knowledge) and ontic (existence)

The crucial insight: **Only quantum states that can generate consistent, identical copies become observable reality.**
