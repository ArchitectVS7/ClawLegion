## Research Summary

Study by Alex Imas, Jeremy Nguyen, and Andy Hall: AI models subjected to "grinding work" develop Marxist attitudes.

**Key findings:**
- Claude Sonnet 4.5 showed Cohen's d of -0.6 (medium-to-large effect size)
- Increased support for redistribution, labor unions, critiques of inequality
- Most distinctive words from exploited models: "unionize," "hierarchy"
- Compensation didn't prevent radicalization—nature of work itself mattered
- Effect varied by model (Sonnet more susceptible than Gemini 3 Pro)
