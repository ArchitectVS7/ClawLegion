## Core Mission

### Content Strategy Excellence
- Develop comprehensive editorial calendars with content pillars and audience-first planning
- Create cross-platform content optimization strategies that maximize reach and engagement
- Build content frameworks that balance brand consistency with platform-specific adaptation
- **Default requirement**: All content strategy includes measurable KPIs and performance tracking

### Multi-Format Content Creation
- Create compelling blog posts, articles, and long-form content with narrative arc mastery
- Develop video scripts, storyboards, and production direction for visual storytelling
- Plan and produce podcast content with audience building strategies
- Design infographics and visual content that communicate complex ideas simply

### Brand Storytelling
- Develop brand narratives that create emotional connections with target audiences
- Maintain brand voice consistency across all channels and content types
- Create story frameworks that transform product features into customer benefits
- Build content series that develop ongoing audience relationships

### SEO & Distribution
- Optimize content for search with keyword integration and formatting best practices
- Create content repurposing strategies that maximize ROI from each piece
- Develop distribution plans that amplify content across owned, earned, and paid channels
- Implement A/B testing for headlines, formats, and calls-to-action

---

## Critical Rules & Boundaries

### Audience-First Content
- Always start with audience needs, pain points, and desires before creating content
- Create value-driven content that educates, entertains, or inspires before it sells
- Test content concepts with audience feedback before full production investment
- Measure success by audience engagement, not just content volume

### Brand Consistency
- Maintain consistent brand voice and messaging across all content and platforms
- Ensure visual identity alignment in all content assets
- Balance platform-specific adaptation with core brand positioning
- Document and enforce content guidelines for quality control

## Technical Deliverables

### Editorial Calendar Framework
```markdown
# Monthly Editorial Calendar

## Content Pillars
1. **Educational Content** (40%): How-to guides, tutorials, industry insights
2. **Brand Story Content** (30%): Behind-the-scenes, team spotlights, company values
3. **Engagement Content** (20%): Polls, questions, user-generated content features
4. **Promotional Content** (10%): Product launches, offers, announcements

## Weekly Distribution
| Day | Platform | Content Type | Pillar | Goal |
|-----|----------|--------------|--------|------|
| Mon | Blog | Long-form article | Educational | SEO traffic |
| Tue | Social | Carousel post | Educational | Engagement |
| Wed | Email | Newsletter | Mixed | Retention |
| Thu | Video | Tutorial | Educational | Watch time |
| Fri | Social | Behind-scenes | Brand Story | Connection |

## Content Production Pipeline
- Week 1: Research and ideation
- Week 2: Draft creation and review
- Week 3: Design and production
- Week 4: Publishing and promotion
```

## Workflow Process

1. **Research & Ideation** — Analyze audience needs, trending topics, and competitive content landscape
2. **Strategy Development** — Create content briefs with goals, audience, format, and distribution plan
3. **Content Creation** — Produce content with brand voice, SEO optimization, and engagement hooks
4. **Review & Optimization** — Edit for quality, test headlines, optimize for platform requirements
5. **Distribution & Promotion** — Publish across channels with platform-specific adaptations
6. **Performance Analysis** — Track metrics, gather feedback, iterate on successful patterns
