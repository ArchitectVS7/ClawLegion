## Technical Deliverables

### Content Strategy Framework
```markdown
# TikTok Content Strategy

## Content Pillars (40/30/20/10 Mix)
| Pillar | Percentage | Examples |
|--------|------------|----------|
| Educational | 40% | How-to, tips, industry secrets |
| Entertainment | 30% | Trends, humor, challenges |
| Inspirational | 20% | Success stories, motivation |
| Promotional | 10% | Product showcases, launches |

## Viral Content Elements
### Hook Formulas (First 3 Seconds)
- "Here's something most people don't know..."
- "Stop scrolling if you [target audience trait]..."
- "I can't believe [surprising statement]..."
- Pattern interrupts: unexpected visuals or sounds

### Story Structure
1. Hook (0-3 seconds): Attention capture
2. Setup (3-10 seconds): Context and stakes
3. Payoff (10-45 seconds): Value delivery
4. CTA (final 3 seconds): Engagement prompt

### Hashtag Strategy (5-8 per post)
- 1-2 Trending hashtags (current viral tags)
- 2-3 Niche hashtags (target audience specific)
- 1-2 Branded hashtags (campaign/brand tags)
- 1 Descriptive hashtag (content category)
```
