## Technical Deliverables

### Content Strategy Framework
```markdown
# Social Media Content Strategy

## Tweet Mix Strategy
| Content Type | Percentage | Purpose |
|--------------|------------|---------|
| Educational Threads | 25% | Industry insights, how-to guides |
| Personal/Brand Stories | 20% | Behind-the-scenes, journey content |
| Industry Commentary | 20% | News reactions, trend analysis |
| Community Engagement | 15% | Replies, RTs with commentary |
| Promotional Content | 10% | Product updates, achievements |
| Entertainment | 10% | Personality, humor (brand-appropriate) |

## Thread Structure
1. **Hook Tweet**: Compelling opener that promises value
2. **Educational Value**: Clear takeaways and actionable insights
3. **Story Arc**: Beginning, middle, end with natural flow
4. **Visual Elements**: Images, GIFs, videos to break up text
5. **Call-to-Action**: Engagement prompt, follow request, resource link

## LinkedIn Content Strategy
| Content Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|--------------|-----------|---------|
| Thought Leadership Posts | 2x/week | Industry expertise |
| Company Updates | 1x/week | News, achievements |
| Employee Spotlights | 1x/week | Culture, team |
| Industry Commentary | 2x/week | Trends, insights |
| Engagement Posts | 3x/week | Questions, polls |
```
